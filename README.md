# Sitter
hi. i am tiny. please download me. a tiny silver robot cat that peeks over your screen and opens all your favorite links at once.

setup instructions:
1. **extract the zip** folder into your downloads or documents.
2. open the folder and double-click **`RunSitter.bat`**.
3. watch your desktop wallpaper. a shortcut icon will appear.
4. click the robot cat, drop your daily links in the box, and hit **save**.
